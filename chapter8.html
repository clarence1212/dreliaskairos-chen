Key Insights: Post-Labor Economy Transformation

Investment Acceleration
AI investment surged 80% in 2024, reaching $100+ billion and representing one-third of all global venture funding.
Workforce Displacement
300 million jobs at risk globally by 2030, requiring massive retraining and transition support programs.
Real Value Creation
Companies like Tesla and Amazon already demonstrate autonomous systems creating substantial economic value without human labor.
Policy Response
UBI trials show mixed results, highlighting need for careful design of post-labor economic support systems.
Global Competition
Nations adopting different strategies: US market-led, EU regulatory, China state-directed approaches to transition.
Timeline Urgency
Post-labor transformation already underway, requiring immediate policy and social adaptation rather than future planning.
